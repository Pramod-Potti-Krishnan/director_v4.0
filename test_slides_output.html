Lessons from Hanuman

Values we can learn from the mighty hero

1

1. Be Devoted & Stay Loyal!

Hanuman showed amazing loyalty to Lord Rama. He always stood by his friends and family, no matter what! Just like Hanuman, we should always be true to the people we care about and never give up on them. Being devoted means being a great friend and a loving family member.

2

2. Be Brave, Face Your Fears!

Hanuman faced many scary challenges, like jumping across the ocean or fighting powerful demons. He didn't let fear stop him! When you feel a little scared, remember Hanuman. Take a deep breath and be brave. You're stronger than you think, and you can overcome anything.

3

3. Be Humble with Great Power

Even though Hanuman was super strong and could do amazing things, he never bragged or showed off. He always used his powers to help others, not to feel important. Being humble means understanding your strengths but always being kind and respecting everyone.

4. Be Helpful to Everyone

Hanuman had unwavering faith in Lord Rama and in his own abilities, even when things looked impossible. This faith gave him the power to achieve the incredible! Believe in yourself, believe in your friends, and believe that good things are possible. Your belief is a superpower!
